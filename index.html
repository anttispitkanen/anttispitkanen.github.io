Coding and web

Coding for me is a way of expressing myself and creating awesome things out of thin air.

I originally took up coding by myself with the help of Codecademy. Eventually I got so carried away that I found myself studying Computer Science at the University of Tampere.

I like to build things that can be seen and interacted with, which is why I'm most into web and front-end development. JavaScript and Node-based technologies are what I use most, along with React.

Visit my Github page to see my code including the source code for this page.

I'm also one of the organizers of the TampereJS meetups.

Community

I believe that people can achieve more when working together than when working alone. That's why I want to bo be a part of community and help the community.

TampereJS

TampereJS is a community of JavaScript and web-development oriented people in Tampere. We organize meetups once a month with companies as sponsors. The community is completely volunteer based and the events are free to attend.

TreStart

TreStart is a community under the city of Tampere where talented people meet local companies to network and to create new business.
