    Community

    I believe that people can achieve more when working together than when working alone. That's why I want to both benefit from being part of a community and give back to the community.


    These are some of the communities I'm involved in:

    TampereJS

    TampereJS is a community of JavaScript and web-development oriented people in Tampere. We organize meetups once a month with companies as sponsors. The community is completely volunteer based and the events are free to attend.

    I volunteered as an organizer for three reasons. First of all I want to learn as much as possible about web development and JavaScript, and what would be a more engaging way. Secondly, I want to create myself a vast network of IT people and companies, and where would that be easier. Thirdly, I saw an opportunity for being involved in creating something new for the Tampere IT scene. For me it's a win-win-win.

    TreStart

    TreStart is a community under the city of Tampere where talented people meet local companies to network and to create new business.

    The services of TreStart are mainly targeted on one hand to highly educated unemployed people, or talents as we call them, and on the other hand to growing companies in the Tampere region. I first found out about TreStart and attended their events when I was unemployed myself. Through the events and actions I got to complete interesting projects with local companies and grow my professional networks with loads of potential employers, business partners and various talents.

    I have gotten much out of attending TreStart events and now I want to help out the community which ever way I can. I still attend all the events possible and try to share my thoughts and tips on job seeking, personal branding and networking.

    Virtual Reality Finland

    VR Finland is an association that supports the development of VR and AR ecosystems in Finland. The goal is to help Finland become the leading player in VR and AR technologies.

    Now that VR and AR are becoming more known and popular I want to be in the frontlines.

    I've joined Virtual Reality Finland fairly recently, but I have already attended several events and even helped organize one.
